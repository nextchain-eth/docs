## **NextChain Hub Ethiopia – Hub Description**

**NextChain Hub Ethiopia** is a community-driven Web3 innovation hub focused on empowering developers, startups, and students to build meaningful blockchain solutions with real-world impact.

As part of the **CATS 2026 Hackathon**, NextChain Hub serves as a local coordination point for participants in Ethiopia, providing structured support, learning opportunities, and a collaborative environment for teams throughout the hackathon journey.

### **What We Offer to Participants**

NextChain Hub supports teams from ideation to refinement by offering:

- **Developer onboarding and orientation sessions**
    
- **Virtual and in-person workshops** focused on blockchain fundamentals and ecosystem tooling
    
- **Technical guidance and mentorship**, particularly around Cardano integration
    
- **Team formation support** for participants who join without a team
    
- **Peer-to-peer collaboration opportunities** within the hub
    
- **Internal review and documentation support** to help teams present their work clearly to evaluators
    

### **Hub Activities & Flow**

- **Pre-submission phase:**  
    Teams are encouraged to form, ideate, and begin submitting their projects before the official deadline.
    
- **Virtual support session (UP TO Dec 15):**  
    Final Q&A, submission guidance, and technical clarification before the registration deadline.
    
- **In-person meetup (Dec 20 – The Hubbits Workspace):**  
    A collaborative session for teams who have already submitted to:
    
    - Meet other teams in the hub
        
    - Discuss and refine their project direction
        
    - Share progress and receive feedback
        
    - Strengthen their concepts beyond the initial submission
        

Teams can continue refining their projects even after submission and share additional updates with the hub.

### **Documentation & Evaluation Support**

NextChain Hub maintains structured **team pages within our Docs system**, ensuring that:

- Each team’s idea, progress, and contribution is clearly documented
    
- Evaluators can easily review team context, approach, and development journey
    
- Teams have the opportunity to update and improve their documentation over time
    

### **Who Should Join NextChain Hub**

- Developers new to blockchain or Cardano
    
- Builders looking for a collaborative learning environment
    
- Teams seeking structured support and accountability
    
- Participants interested in refining their ideas beyond the first submission
    

### **Our Goal**

Our goal is to help teams build **strong, well-documented, and impactful projects**, while fostering a supportive developer community that continues beyond the hackathon.

NextChain Hub Ethiopia is committed to collaboration, learning, and long-term ecosystem growth.
