## Team Overview

**GreenSoil** is a sustainable agriculture project focused on improving soil health and crop productivity through **liquid natural fertilizer**, designed specifically for small to medium-scale farmers.

The project addresses soil degradation, rising input costs, and environmental damage caused by synthetic fertilizers. By combining eco-friendly agricultural practices with **Cardano-based traceability**, GreenSoil introduces transparency, trust, and long-term sustainability into agricultural input systems.

## Problem Context

Many farmers in Ethiopia rely heavily on synthetic fertilizers, which degrade soil health, harm beneficial microorganisms, and increase long-term costs. Sustainable alternatives exist but often lack trust, visibility, and consistent adoption.

This creates a cycle of declining soil fertility, environmental damage, and reduced agricultural resilience.
