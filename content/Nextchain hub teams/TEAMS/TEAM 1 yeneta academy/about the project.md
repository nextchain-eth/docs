## Problem Context

Many learners in Ethiopia and across Africa face:

- Limited access to quality, affordable education
    
- Difficulty proving skills and learning outcomes
    
- Low motivation due to unclear career pathways
    
- Disconnection between learning and real opportunities
    

Traditional education systems struggle to provide **verifiable skills, incentives, and inclusive access**.

---

## Project Vision

Yeneta Academy envisions a **decentralized, learner-centered education ecosystem** where:

- Learning achievements are verifiable and portable
    
- Learners are rewarded for progress and contribution
    
- Skills connect directly to real-world opportunities
    
- Education is inclusive, practical, and future-ready
