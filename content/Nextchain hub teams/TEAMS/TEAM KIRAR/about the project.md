## Problem Context

Creators across emerging markets rely on centralized platforms that control distribution, monetization, and ownership. Revenue sharing is opaque, royalties are delayed or unfair, and creators lack direct relationships with their audiences.

These systems limit cultural expression, economic opportunity, and long-term sustainability for creative communities.

---

## Project Vision

Kirar envisions a decentralized creator ecosystem where artists, writers, and filmmakers own their content, earn transparently, and engage directly with supporters.

Through NFTs, smart contracts, and community participation, Kirar enables fair value distribution, shared ownership models, and sustainable creative economies.
