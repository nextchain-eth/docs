## Problem Context

Smallholder farmers across Ethiopia and Africa face **limited access to affordable credit and green subsidies**. While many adopt climate-smart and sustainable farming practices, traditional financial and institutional systems **cannot reliably verify these behaviors**.

As a result:

- Farmers remain under-supported
    
- Sustainable practices are not incentivized
    
- Green finance lacks trusted data
    
- Investment and policy decisions are fragmented
