## Problem Context

Ethiopia‚Äôs employment system is largely **paper-based, fragmented, and manually verified**, making it difficult to trust credentials, skills, and work history. This results in slower hiring, higher fraud risk, and limited access to fair employment‚Äîespecially for informal workers and early-career professionals.

---

## Project Vision

VirusG envisions a **trusted digital employment ecosystem** where individuals own verifiable professional identities and employers can confidently validate skills, experience, and credentials‚Äîwithout relying on centralized or opaque systems.

---

## Cardano Alignment

Cardano serves as the project‚Äôs **trust and verification layer**, enabling:

- **Verifiable digital identities and credentials**
    
- **Immutable work history and qualification records**
    
- **Interoperability across institutions and employers**
    
- **Low-cost, sustainable infrastructure suitable for national scale**
    

The project complements Ethiopia‚Äôs **Fayda digital ID initiative** by extending trusted identity into employment and workforce data, aligning with Cardano‚Äôs focus on **identity, governance, and real-world infrastructure**.
